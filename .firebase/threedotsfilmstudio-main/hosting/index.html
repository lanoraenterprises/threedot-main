About Us

Founded in 2010 in the heart of Kochi, Threedots Film Studio began with a singular vision: to provide the infrastructure where creativity meets technical excellence. What started as a dedicated studio floor has evolved over 15 years into one of Kerala's most comprehensive hubs for professional photography and cinematography.

Our history is defined by a commitment to "firsts." From introducing Kerala’s first ARRI Alexa XT and RED Epic-X to housing India’s first set of Cooke S8/i lenses, we have consistently pushed the boundaries of what’s possible in regional production. Today, our Edappally facility offers three specialized studio floors, designed to meet the rigorous demands of modern high-end shoots.

At Threedots, we don’t just provide gear; we provide a legacy of precision. Backed by expert technical assistants and a world-class inventory, we ensure that every frame you capture is supported by the best tools in the industry.

Our Story

2010

Inception

Founded in Kochi with a simple yet powerful vision—to create a space where creativity and technical excellence move in perfect sync. Started as Kochi’s largest photography studio of its time.

2011

Expansion

A second studio floor was added, strengthening Threedots as a trusted professional workspace.

2013

Cinema Systems

Expanded into cinema camera systems, introducing industry firsts to Kerala: ARRI Alexa XT, RED Epic-X, Alura Zoom lenses, and OConnor heads.

2015

The Edappally Facility

Launch of a purpose-built studio facility in Edappally, the first in Kerala with multiple studio floors (4000, 1400, & 1100 sq. ft.) in a single complex.

Recent

Constant Evolution

Profoto systems (2019), Fujifilm GFX (2021), and India’s first Cooke S8/i lenses & Broncolor systems (2023).
