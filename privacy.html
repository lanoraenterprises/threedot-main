Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Three Dots Studio ("we," "our," or "us"). We match the finest in cinematography, visual effects, and storytelling technology. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and register for our events.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you strictly provide to us when you register for the event. This includes:

  • Personal identification information (Name, email address, phone number).
  • Professional details (Portfolio URLs, Student ID proofs).
  • Payment transaction details (via our secure payment partners).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process your registration and issue your event badge.
  • Communicate with you regarding event updates and schedules.
  • Verify your eligibility for specific passes (e.g., Student or Pro).
  • Improve our event planning and logistics.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect the security of your personal information. However, please note that no electronic transmission over the Internet or information storage technology can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions or comments about this policy, you may contact us at hello@threedots.com.
